Why we get Health Insurance?

Health insurance is obtained for various crucial reasons, making it an essential component of personal financial planning. Firstly, it provides financial protection against the high costs of medical care. Without health insurance, individuals may face significant financial strain from medical expenses related to accidents, illnesses, or chronic conditions. Health insurance helps mitigate these costs by covering a portion of medical bills, including hospital stays, doctor's visits, prescription medications, and preventive care services.
