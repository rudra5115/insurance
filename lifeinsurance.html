Why we get Life Insurance?

Life insurance is obtained for several critical reasons, making it a cornerstone of financial planning for many individuals. Firstly, it serves as a means of providing financial security and support to loved ones in the event of the policyholder's death. Life insurance proceeds can help cover funeral expenses, outstanding debts, mortgage payments, and day-to-day living expenses, ensuring that dependents are not burdened with financial hardship during a difficult time. Moreover, life insurance can act as income replacement, offering a source of financial stability for surviving family members, especially if the deceased was the primary breadwinner.
