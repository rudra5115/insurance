Why we get Car Insurance?

Car insurance is a fundamental necessity for drivers due to its multifaceted benefits. Firstly, it ensures compliance with legal mandates prevalent in most regions, mandating drivers to be insured to operate their vehicles lawfully. Beyond legal obligations, car insurance serves as a crucial financial safety net, shielding drivers from substantial financial losses arising from accidents, theft, or damage to their vehicles. Moreover, it extends coverage to medical expenses, providing reassurance by addressing injuries sustained by the driver and passengers.
